Full_Promoter_Dataset.mat contains the source data used to generate manuscript figures 1-5 and figures EV1-5.

Full_Promoter_Dataset.mat = p53 modulation dataset with MDM2/CDKN1A promoter reporters

The mat file includes:

raw_measurements – full dataset which is organized as follows: {promoter,treatment}{individual cell, channel}(times)

promoter_list – MDM2 or CDKN1A

channel_list – RFP or YFP

treatment_list – 5uM_high amplitude, high frequency, long duration, 15uM_low amplitude, low frequency, or natural frequency

times – 73 timepoints acquired at 20 min intervals for 24hrs
