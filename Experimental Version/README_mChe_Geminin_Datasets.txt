mChe-Geminin Dataset files contain the source data used to generate manuscript figure 6.

mChe-Geminin dataset_natural and low frequency expts.mat = Exposure of CDKN1A promoter reporter cell line with 
mChe-geminin reporter to low and natural p53 frequencies

mChe-Geminin dataset_high frequency expt.mat = Exposure of CDKN1A promoter reporter cell line with 
mChe-geminin reporter to high p53 frequency


mChe-Geminin dataset_natural and low frequency expts.mat includes:

raw_measurements_geminin – full dataset which is organized as follows: {promoter,treatment}{individual cell, channel}(times)

promoter_list – CDKN1A

channel_list – RFP or YFP

treatment_list – low frequency or natural frequency

times – 120 timepoints acquired at 20 min intervals for 40hrs


mChe-Geminin dataset_high frequency expt.mat includes:

raw_measurements_geminin – full dataset which is organized as follows: {promoter,treatment}{individual cell, channel}(times)

promoter_list – CDKN1A

channel_list – RFP or YFP

treatment_list – high frequency

times – 121 timepoints acquired at 20 min intervals for 40hrs
